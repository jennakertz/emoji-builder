The Emoji Builder is a 2016 Spark Philadelphia project. The concept and design of this website is credited to my Spark buddy, Le'Naya.

We met for two hours each week over the course of eight weeks to produce what you see today. We hope you enjoy :)

About us

It was a typical Wednesday when Jenna and Le'Naya signed up for the Spark program. Little did they know they would have lots in common. They both loved emojis, art, the internet, and food! Soon they decided to combine their passions and create a website. And thus the Emoji Builder was born. Enjoy!

Emoji library

We made the library to show what we can do! We mainly used girl emojis to send a message to people ( mainly boys) that girls can do more than just dance, sing, etc.
